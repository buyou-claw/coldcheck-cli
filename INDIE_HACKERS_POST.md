# Just launched ColdCheck CLI — AI-powered cold email analyzer that runs locally

Hey IH community!

Today I'm launching ColdCheck CLI — an AI-powered tool that analyzes cold emails and tells you if they'll work before you send them.

**The backstory:** I've been doing outbound for my previous startup. Sending 50-100 cold emails a day. Response rate was brutal — maybe 2-3%. I kept wondering: is it the product? The list? The timing? Or just... the email itself?

Turns out, mostly the email.

**What I built:** A CLI tool that takes any cold email and returns:
- Overall score (1-10)
- Subject line analysis
- Personalization depth
- Value proposition clarity
- CTA effectiveness
- Length assessment
- Top 3 specific improvement suggestions

**The key insight:** Cold emails fail not because your product is bad, but because 90% of cold emails are generic, vague, and forgettable. A 6/10 email gets ignored. An 8/10 gets a response.

**Numbers:**
- Time to build: 2 days
- Lines of code: ~400 (Python)
- Users before launch: 0 (you're it)
- Current MRR: $0 (free tool, leads to cloud version later)

**Why free + local:**
- No server means no deployment, no infrastructure costs
- Emails stay on your machine (privacy)
- Low barrier to try — just `pip install`

**Lessons learned:**
1. The best way to validate an idea is to actually build it and put it in front of people
2. Constraints breed creativity — "runs locally" forced a cleaner architecture
3. The hardest part isn't building, it's knowing what to build next
